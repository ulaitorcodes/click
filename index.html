CREATIVITY is infallibly our special effect when strategising to see that the implementation of our vision is a reality. Amidst the oddities we readily find lingering in almost every sector, we show ingenuity when we create models that solve the necessary problems.

About us

IThe center for Leadership Innovation and Creative Knowledge is a platform for the development and advancement of Africa’s solution Architects who are daily fixing Africa’s challenges with African inspired solutions. It is a network of leaders with a desire to see change no matter how small yet significant in our communities. It is our belief trial the Africa of our dreams is the Africa that takes the lead in harnessing its God’s given natural resources for the improvement of our way of life and advancement of technology to lead the resurgence of the Lions of Africa.
