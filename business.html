About CLICK

C.L.I.C.K PROFILE The center for Leadership Innovation and Creative Knowledge is a platform for the development and advancement of Africa’s solution Architects who are daily fixing Africa’s challenges with African inspired solutions.

It is a network of leaders with a desire to see change no matter how small yet significant in our communities. It is our belief trial the Africa of our dreams is the Africa that takes the lead in harnessing its God’s given natural resources for the improvement of our way of life and advancement of technology to lead the resurgence of the Lions of Africa.

Join us

Our Vision

VISION Our vision is to be recognized as a leading innovative platform in providing global leadership in tackling of African challenges thereby conquering the mindset of impossibilities. In C.L.I.C.K. developing leaders isn’t just about putting people through programs but working critically to support them in the right organizational context: – a workplace, environment and community that encourages sharing, risk taking and growth. C.L.I.C.K. is that Leadership Center for Innovation and Creative Knowledge powered by CLIQUES, thereby breaking silos and establishing partnership with other groups pursuing similar outcome in the area of innovative leadership.
